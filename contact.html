Ask Us Directly
Do you provide free quotes?
Yes, we offer free, no-obligation quotes for all our garden maintenance, landscaping, and exterior cleaning services. Just contact us to arrange a visit.
How quickly can you start a project?
Our availability varies depending on the season and current workload. However, we strive to schedule initial consultations and start work as swiftly as possible. Please contact us for our current lead times.
Are you fully insured for large estate work?
Yes, Lavender Row is fully insured to handle projects of all sizes, from small domestic gardens to extensive country estates.
Do you remove all garden waste?
Yes, as part of our garden clearance and maintenance services, we ensure all green waste is responsibly removed and disposed of, leaving your property clean and tidy.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance contracts?
Absolutely. We offer tailored, regular maintenance plans to ensure your garden remains in pristine condition year-round.
What equipment do you use for exterior cleaning?
We use professional-grade pressure washing and exterior cleaning equipment designed to safely and effectively remove dirt, algae, and grime from patios, driveways, and other hard surfaces without causing damage.
